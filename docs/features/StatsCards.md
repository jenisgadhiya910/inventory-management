# Stats Cards (Inventory Overview)

## What It Does

Stats cards sit at the top of the dashboard and give you a quick snapshot of your inventory health. Each card highlights one key number so you can spot issues at a glance without digging into tables or reports.

---

## The Cards

### 1. Total Products
- Shows the total number of products in your inventory
- Includes a small indicator showing how many were added this month

### 2. Low Stock Items
- Shows how many products are below their minimum stock level
- Highlighted in amber/orange to draw attention
- Clicking this card takes you to the filtered product list showing only low stock items

### 3. Out of Stock Items
- Shows how many products have zero quantity
- Highlighted in red as a warning
- Clicking this card takes you to the filtered product list showing only out-of-stock items

### 4. Total Inventory Value
- Shows the total value of all your stock (quantity multiplied by cost price for each product)
- Displayed in your configured currency
- Includes a small indicator showing value change compared to last month

---

## Additional Stats (Below Main Cards)

### 5. Categories Count
- Total number of active product categories

### 6. Active Suppliers
- Total number of suppliers currently marked as active

### 7. Items to Reorder
- Number of products that need to be restocked (below minimum level but not yet at zero)

### 8. Average Profit Margin
- The average margin across all products (selling price minus cost price, as a percentage)

---

## Visual Details

- Each card has a title, a large number, and a small trend indicator
- Trend indicators show an up or down arrow with a percentage change
  - Green arrow up = positive trend
  - Red arrow down = negative trend
- Cards that represent warnings (low stock, out of stock) have colored left borders to stand out
- All numbers update automatically when you add, edit, or restock products

---

## Responsive Behavior

| Screen Size | What happens |
|---|---|
| Desktop | 4 main cards in a row, additional stats below |
| Tablet | 2 cards per row |
| Mobile | Cards stack into a single column |
